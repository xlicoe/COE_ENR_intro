Program Highlights

Premier Facilities: Peterson Hall of Science features over $3 million in advanced instrumentation, giving undergraduates hands-on experience rare for a small college environment.

National Recognition: Coe is a long-standing host for the National Science Foundation (NSF) REU program, supporting elite summer research opportunities.

Proven Outcomes: Graduates secure positions at industry leaders like Google, Tesla, and NASA, or pursue advanced degrees with full funding at MIT, Harvard, and Stanford.

Unique in Iowa: Coe offers the only Engineering Physics major in the state, bridging pure science with practical engineering application.
